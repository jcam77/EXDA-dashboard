Depending on deployment and use, processing is based on one or more of:

- Art. 6(1)(b): performance of a contract (providing requested functionality)

- Art. 6(1)(a): consent (where required, especially for optional integrations)
- Art. 6(1)(c): legal obligation (if applicable)
